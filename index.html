Our Mission

Why We Exist

Our school is small and rural, and students rarely get science-focused field trips. STEM students rarely get access to labs, research centers, wildlife programs, or tech companies. This club gives students real-world experiences they wouldn’t get otherwise.

Building Skills

We focus on building leadership, planning, teamwork, and communication skills that matter for high school, college, and life. It creates a fun and meaningful way for students to explore science outside the classroom.

Trip Teams

We use Trip Teams to keep everyone engaged and learning. Roles are refreshed for every trip to keep things new.

Note Takers

Write down important facts, observations, and things the group learned.

Photographers

Take pictures of exhibits, equipment, wildlife, or demonstrations.

Presentation Creators

Build a simple recap slide using the group’s notes and photos.

How to Make a Trip Team Slide

1
Click on one of the template images.
2
Click Replace Image and upload your team's amazing photos.
3
Add your group members' names at the top of the slide.
4
Where the template text is, put in the exciting details & facts about your trip!
